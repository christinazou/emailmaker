email maker

When I first came to America, writing english email became vital part of my life. I spent a huge amount of time choosing my words, only hoping that the email looks normal. That's why I made this little tool.

Most emails follow this structure:

greeting

contexts of what you are going to say.

your subject

some detail about your subject

greeting again

Although their structure is similar, formal emails and informal emails have a huge difference. So I made seperate webpage for them.
